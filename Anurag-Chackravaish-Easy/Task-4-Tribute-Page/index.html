CR7

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Saudi Professional League club Al Nassr and the Portugal national team.

Career Highlights

Ballon d'Or Awards

Cristiano Ronaldo has won 5 Ballon d'Or awards, recognizing him as the best player in the world on multiple occasions.

European Golden Shoes

He has claimed 4 European Golden Shoes, awarded to the leading goalscorer in league matches from the top division of every European national league.

UEFA Champions League

He is the all-time top scorer in the UEFA Champions League, showcasing his impact in Europe's most prestigious club competition.

Career Goals

Ronaldo has scored over 700 career goals, an incredible feat that highlights his consistency and prowess as a forward.
